Madrid Dam Music Series goes on the road to Maplewood Health Care and Rehabilitation.

In the spring of 2019, we brought a formal gala to the residents of Maplewood, with entertainment by the fabulous Blooms Brothers. All formal clothing was loaned for the event by the music series. A similar event will be announced for spring of 2020.

Repeat Performance.

This year 2020 will mark the 2nd annual Repeat Performance Formal Wear event pop-up store! We were honored to give away and/or lend almost 300 dresses and men’s formal wear. This year our stock of womens and mens formal wear will be greatly increased.

All MDMS events are family friendly....no smoking, alcohol, or strobe lighting.
