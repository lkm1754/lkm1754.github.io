COVID-19 Safety Precautions

NYS and SLC COVID-19 safety requirements will be in place. All performances will be seen from the Park Pavilion. We invite you to fill your car with as many people as seat belts and to your own COVID-19 protocol. The parking lot will have a limited number of spaces open. Everyone will remain in their vehicle. All audience members need to be with a vehicle. Open seating on the lawn will not be permitted. We invite you to bring your own snacks and soft drinks. MDMS will not offer food, beverages, raffles, or auctions.
